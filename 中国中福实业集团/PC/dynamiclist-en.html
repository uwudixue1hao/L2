Renowned Hong Kong Stock Commentators visit Fu Shou Yuan 2014.05.29 0:00 Since Fu Shou Yuan's successful listing in the Hong Kong capital market last December, investors have shown keen interest in the Company's development prospect and cultural connotations. Eight well-known stock commentators from Hong Kong attended a two-day field trip of Shanghai Fu Shou Yuan on May 24th. These stock commentators are very influential in Hong Kong's capital market and enjoy high reputation in stock-picking. They are Chan Wing Luk, Kwok Sze Chi, Lin King Ham, Kwok Ka Yiu, Pun Tit Shan, Lok Siu Kee, Lam Ka Kei and Lee Wai Nam. Representatives of Fu Shou Yuan, namely Chairman Mr. BAI Xiaojiang, General Manager Mr. WANG Jisheng, Deputy General Manager Ms. YI Hua, Deputy General Manager Mr. ZHAO Yu and Chief Financial Officer Mr. LUK Wai Keung extended warm welcome to these distinguished guests and made a detailed presentation of Fu Shou Yuan's operation strategies and cultural philosophy. Through site visit, these stock commentators acquired an in-depth understanding of the corporate fundamentals of Fu Shou Yuan. They further expressed strong interest and recognition in the Company's business and prospects. They were also highly impressed by the management team's enthusiastic working attitude, vision and ambition.

Renowned Hong Kong stock commentators are showing strong interest in Fu Shou Yuan Humanism Memorial Park in Shanghai

During the welcome dinner, Chairman BAI Xiaojiang spoke about the changes in Shanghai, history of death care service industry in the PRC and Fu Shou Yuan's development path over the past two decades. The stock commentators were amazed by both rapid development of the death care service industry in the PRC and the growth of Fu Shou Yuan. Mr. BAI also mentioned that when Hong Kong S.A.R Government Department of Health visited Fu Shou Yuan, the Company's service philosophy and site planning were highly recognized by the Director Mr. Chow Yat-ngok at that time. Mr. Chow welcomed the Company to explore Hong Kong market. Mr. BAI said, after years of experience and preparation, the Company has planned to launch business in Shenzhen and grasp the opportunities after successful listing in the Stock Exchange of Hong Kong Limited ("HKEx"). The Company has been keen to provide high-quality services to more people, as well as convey innovative corporate culture that has stressed the importance of humanistic care as well as cultural and artistic pursuits to Hong Kong.
