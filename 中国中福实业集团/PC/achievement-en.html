Welfare Industry

Welfare Industry – Funeral Service

Zhongfu attaches great significance to welfare industry, and makes great contribution to Chinese welfare industry by exploring a new path on funeral service and aged care industry.

AchievementFu Shou Yuan

Fu Shou Yuan International Group Ltd., invested and established by Zhongfu, is a first-class modern cemetery in China, which opens up the era of modern funeral culture and social and human heritage. Presently, Fu Shou Yuan has become a famous model in funeral industry and formed an innovative mode. It is selected as a model base by national Civil Affairs, Ministry of Construction, and Ministry of Culture. Its branches can be found across the county, and it has become the first listed company among Chinese funeral industry.

Fu Shou Yuan is the biggest provider of funeral service, as well as the leader and integrator in domestic market. It is also a place filled with memories and emotions, a company with traditional values and a social citizen with responsibility.

It provides professional, customized and diversified service to meet different customer demands. It also helps people to find warmth and strength from the memory of past life so as to create a better and happy life through humanity memorial.

Its mission for the next two decades is to "break through tradition and enlighten future". It will make use of its brand and leading position in the market to expand funeral service in more regions, extend vertical service range, and set up industrial standard. On the basis of traditional culture and consciousness, Fu Shou Yuan will continue to promote international cooperation and exchanges, introduce better funeral services, and create a new funeral service model in future.

Welfare Industry –Aged Care Industry

Zhongfu explores the construction and development of pension industry actively. It cooperates with Peking Union Medical College, Japan Tokushukai Medical Group, SECOM Group, John Knox Village, Laguna Woods Village, professional teams on aged community and Gerontology Department of University of North Texas, and learn from them with the purpose to meet the cultural requirements of Chinese pension industry. This project is attached great importance by the central government, and great support from Ministry of Civil Affairs, Ministry of Health, China Committee on Aging, China Aging Development Foundation and China Charity Federation. It is also one of the national "Love Care" projects.

Urban Investment Construction

Zhongfu pays much attention to urban investment and construction. In recent years, it has participated in many key projects in Shanghai, Chongqing and other cities. These projects, in both investment and financing, and quality and characteristic, demonstrate modern scientific concept and far-reaching social influence.

AchievementShanghai Lupu Bridge

Zhongfu co-established Shanghai Lupu Bridge Investment and Development Co., Ltd. as a major sponsor. It conformed to the requirements of national investment and financing system reform, integrated resource advantages of 6 large-scale companies, and participated in the construction and management of major Shanghai municipal projects. Lupu Bridge, with a total investment of 2.5 billion RMB, and its great curves and modeling of combination of curves and straight lines, becomes a new international landmark of Shanghai in the 21st century.

Real Estate

Real Estate - Development

Since its establishment in 1992, Zhongfu real estate industry has been actively involved in Shanghai old city reconstruction, and has made great contribution to the improvement of urban environment and residents' living conditions. In recent years, it has completed a development area of over 3 million square meters, including residential real estate, commercial real estate, industrial real estate, etc. Since 2003, its Zhongfu Property Co., Ltd. has been selected as Shanghai 50 Best Development Companies for many times.

AchievementPhase I Zhongfu City, Phase II , Phase III

Block 1, Block 2 and Block 3 of Zhongfu City were completed in December 2005, and presented Hectometer Champs Elysees with Shanghai features and middle- and high-end residential real estate on Nanjing Road, next to the Pedestrian Street.

AchievementShanghai Zhongfu Hotel Management Co., Ltd.

In the most prosperous Shanghai Bund and Nanjing Road areas, Shanghai Zhongfu Hotel Management Co., Ltd. owns two famous hotels: Salvo Hotel Shanghai and Charms Hotel.

Salvo Hotel Shanghai

A large-scale high-star business hotel affiliated to Shanghai Zhongfu and fully operated by Shanghai Zhongfu Hotel Management Co., Ltd. It is located in Shanghai Bund, the most prosperous business circle in Huangpu District, and on 339 Guangdong Road, the junction of East Yan'an Road and Middle Shandong Road, with walking distance to Bund, Yuyuan Garden and Nanjing Road Walking Street. It has been open for business since 2004.

Salvo Hotel Shanghai features dignified and elegant architectural style, combining Chinese and western styles. It has rich and grandeurs atmosphere as well as modern feelings. The hotel is consisted of main and auxiliary structures. The main building is 31-storey, with a total floor area of approximately 50,000 square meters. It has over 600 "digital" business rooms, deluxe rooms and others, as well as multifunctional conference hall which can accommodate 250 people and a number of different conference rooms with different specification. Salvo Hotel Shanghai, with its excellent quality and sincere service, will impress you with great value and pleasant memories.

Shanghai Charms Hotel

The hotel is close to Nanjing Road Pedestrian Street, with walking distance to Bund and other major tourist destinations. The hotel is well equipped with functional and fashionable rooms, and elegant and cozy restaurant and lobby. It is your best choice no matter you want to have dinner, or hold parties and banquets. Its fully equipped conferences rooms are of different sizes. This 26-storey building has 520 guest rooms. The hotel opened in 2002.

Real Estate - Planning and Design

Shanghai Zhongfu Architectural Design Institute is a Class A design organization, with Grade I qualification in design, decoration and supervision. Its projects have been selected as excellent works many a time by provincial and municipal authorities. And it provides design service nationwide.

AchievementsLujiazui Financial Tower

Lujiazui Financial Tower has a total construction area of 69,403 square meters, with 19 floors above ground and 3 underground. Every floor features wide vision over Huangpu River. It also has a sunken plaza, providing business support services, such as catering and leisure. It is located in north of Lujiazui Ring Road, adjacent to Oriental Pearl Tower, Shanghai Ocean Aquarium and Super Brand Mall.

The building is in rectangle, with energy-efficient glass curtain wall in the outside, simple and neat with no redundant decoration. The inside patterns and architectural standard conform with Grade A standard, and meet the requirements of international financial institutions and enterprises that intend to enter the Lujiazui financial district.

AchievementsLianyang Commercial Plaza

Located in Lianyang community in Central Pudong, Lianyang Commercial Plaza is adjacent to Thumb Plaza. The plaza is the central place of four buildings: Lianyang Department Store, a restaurant, an entertainment location and an iBiz business hotel. In architectural style, it features coexistence of modern and traditional characteristics. In architectural form and detail treatment approach, it reflects ArtDeco of Shanghai culture. It integrates Shanghai culture with modern business concept, creating a public commercial space containing traditional and modern elements, which interaction helps people to appreciate the charm of modern Shanghai.

Trade and Logistics Services

Comprehensive Trade

Zhongfu trading system has comprehensive import and export rights approved by the state, and its business scope includes: grain and oil, food, soil and livestock, textile products, garments, light industry, artware, ferrous metals, non-ferrous metals, non-metallic mineral products, chemical and petrochemical products, rubber products, pharmaceuticals, medical equipment, transport equipment, machinery and equipment, instruments and other products that are permitted by national policy.

Zhongfu has operations in over 60 countries and regions around the world, with close long-term cooperation with about 10 thousand traders. Apart from traditional trade, it also has bulk trade relations with foreign countries in petrochemicals, energy, materials, ore, etc.

Modern Logistics

Zhongfu has a modern logistics system, integrating warehousing, distribution, freight forwarding, sea transport and container transport. It also sets up Shanghai Eastern Fuda Transport Service Company together with DHL Danzas Air & Ocean. and China Eastern Airlines. This joint venture has Grade A international freight qualification, and is specialized in air cargo agent. It, together with Shanghai Zhongfu International Freight Co., Ltd., forms the core strength of Zhongfu logistics system.

Modern Technology Investment Service

Zhongfu attaches great significance to investment and construction of modern science and technology service industry, especially life sciences and communications technology. It is actively involved in innovation and cutting-edge technology.

Capital

Zhongfu focuses on stock market investment, futures trading, business investment, mergers, acquisitions and restructuring and asset management, with the investment, mergers, acquisitions and restructuring of high-tech business having first priority. It has already successfully operated the listing of bus share and Fu Shou Yuan. Presently, it is operating Shanghai Lupu Bridge Investment Development Co., Ltd., and investing in securities companies and insurance companies. It invests and sets up Chongqing Yuzhong Small-scale loans Co., Ltd. to enter financial industry.
