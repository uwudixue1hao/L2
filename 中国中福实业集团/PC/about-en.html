About Us

China Zhongfu Group was established with the approval from central Government, with Zhongfu Industries Corporation as our parent company. Based in Shanghai, Zhongfu provides services nationwide and worldwide, and takes social responsibility. It is a modern group featuring multi-directional opening, international operation and diversified development. In 2009, Zhongfu was honored as "Shanghai Famous Trademark".

Established in 1995, Zhongfu Group was originally known as Kanghua. In 2000, it was restructured. After two decades, Zhongfu now has an asset of over 30 billion RMB, leading over 40 domestic and foreign companies, including public-listed companies. In recent years, its business scope has been expanded to trade, logistics, construction, capital operation, high-tech, philanthropy, etc. through modern business operational concept and advanced management methodology, by integrating the advantages of domestic and foreign resources. It has preliminarily formed the interaction matrix of three drives, where big trading drives big capital, big capital drives big investment; therefore, it develops to a benign circulation.

History

  • 1990

    In 1990, we cooperated with Shanghai Public Bus Company and established Zhongfu Bus Associated Company, which started a new bus operation mode featuring quality services, superior management and socialized operation. It is highly praised by Shanghai Municipal Government and Shanghai people. In 1993, this company was listed in Stock Market A-board in China after restructuring (Bus Group).

  • 1994

    In 1994, we invested 100 million RMB together with Continental Grain Company and Sinopec Shanghai Petrochemical Company Limited on LPG operation projects to improve residents and industrial energy structure and improve urban air quality.

    In 1994, we built Shanghai Fu Shou Yuan, the pioneering cemetery park to reform funeral industry in China. With "After-life Garden" as its operation philosophy, Fu Shou Yuan combines humanity philosophy with traditional Chinese funeral culture and forms modern garden culture, integrating landscape, history, culture, education and leisure, which has become a benchmark for cemeteries in China.

  • 1995

    In 1995, we planned the first batch of land to lease in Shanghai Hongqiao Development Zone to foreign investors, where the lease in batches was gaining steam, and the company made a great contribution to the construction of Shanghai Hongqiao Development Zone.

  • 1998

    In 1998, we participated in the reconstruction project of the old downtown in central Shanghai - Huangpu District and Xuhui District, and solved the urban reconstruction problems fundamentally with the new concept of "remove and return". It's a model for Shanghai downtown urban reconstruction.

  • 2000

    In 2000, we started Shanghai Lupu Bridge Investment and Development Co., Ltd. as a major initiator with a total investment of 2.5 billion RMB. The company was established to invest and construct of "the Top 1 Steel Arch Bridge" and for a management period of 25 years. This bridge was awarded a number of international architectural prizes, and has become a landmark of Shanghai.

  • 2001

    In 2001, we carried out development and construction of large steel-structure housing in Shanghai jointly with Laiwu Steel Group Co., Ltd. serving as a pioneer in the further development and upgrading of new materials, technologies and processes in Shanghai housing industry.

  • 2003

    In 2003, we established Chongqing Caiyuanba Yangtze River Bridge Construction Development Co., Ltd., with an investment of 2.5 billion RMB, for the investment and construction of Caiyuanba Yangtze River Bridge with BOT mode.

  • 2006

    In 2006, we took "Shanghai Environment Protection" central mall development pilot project, and established a joint venture -- Shanghai Central Mall Investment Co., Ltd. together with Shanghai New World Holding Co., Ltd. It invested approximately 2.5 billion RMB to complete replacement and relocation. Huangpu District Government invited investment and merchants from domestic and foreign markets.

  • 2008

    In 2008, we invested and set up aged communities in Fengxian District and Baoshan District of Shanghai and an explored sustainable aged care model with Chinese culture through intensive industrial experiences.

  • 2011

    In December 2011, we established Shanghai Zhongfu Group International Trade Co., Ltd. and began to explore the development of international trading.

  • 2013

    In 2013, Fu Shou Yuan International Group Co., Ltd. went public in Hong Kong, being the first funeral business stock listed in Hong Kong main board.

Corporate Culture

Corporate Spirit and Business Principles

Corporate Spirit: pioneering, hard working, practical, innovative;
Business Principles: six industries, three drives,; diversified, international, and modernized;
With "Zhong (Chinese) Rules the Business Market, Fu(Benefit) the Whole World", ranks in the world's famous companies with its reputation and strength.

Vision

In face of new economic era, and in order to adapt to global economic integration, and grasp the opportunities and deals with the challenges brought by China's joining in WTO, Zhongfu establishes a flat, facilitated, networked and informationized management system on the basis of organizational innovation and process reengineering, and builds core competitiveness in capital organization, marketing organization and human resources organization in worldwide. Rely on science, technology and innovation, it realizes rapid appreciation and expansion of capital, and further expands development space to achieve strategic goal of extraordinary and great-leap-forward development, and greater performance.

With the concept of "learning, thinking, research, innovation", Zhongfu will integrate global resource, continuously enhance and improve business management. With the mission of benefiting the society, it will contribute to the development of our nation and the society and promote harmonious development of company, employees and the society as a whole.
